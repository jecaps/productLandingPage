Dripping with legends of beasts and witches, the dense, dark, forested hills of the Black Forest are endlessly beguiling. Walks in the Black Forest place you in the magical heart of this picturesque, fairy-tale region, where trails criss-cross a sublime expanse of high hills, atmospheric lakes and gorgeous woodland. Traditional medieval towns and villages act as charming bases for your adventures. Wake up to the sound of the cuckoo clock in an archetypal half-timbered guesthouse and stride out onto ancient paths under the brooding, evergreen canopy. Magnificent all year round, in the summer you’ll be glad of the shade, whilst in winter you’ll be lured onwards by the bewitching white wonderland. Discover deep, overgrown gorges; majestic, tumbling waterfalls and astonishing views of the Rhine Valley and the distant Alps. Hikes in the Black Forest take you to truly enchanting, unforgettable places. It’s a hikers’ fantasy land and you’d be cuckoo to miss it.
